Welcome to the Battle of the Elements, an Earthy Version of the Classic Rock, Paper, Scissors Game!

🛋 Settle in and play awhile. 🪴

You can choose between the Classic or Challenge versions of the game.

If you choose the Classic Game, you will pick one of 3 elemental weapons:

🔥 Fire, 🌏 Earth, or 🌊 Water

🔥 Fire ~beats~ 🌏 Earth, 🌏 Earth ~beats~ 🌊 Water, and 🌊 Water ~beats~ 🔥 Fire

If you choose the Challenge Game, you will pick one of 5 elemental weapons:

🔥 Fire, 🌏 Earth, 🌊 Water, 💨 Wind, or ⚡️ Ether

🔥 Fire ~beats~ 🌏 Earth and 🌊 Water

🌏 Earth ~beats~ 🌊 Water and 💨 Wind

🌊 Water ~beats~ 💨 Wind and ⚡️ Ether

💨 Wind ~beats~ ⚡️ Ether and 🔥 Fire

⚡️ Ether ~beats~ 🔥 Fire and 🌏 Earth
